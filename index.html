An Open Letter to the Person I Love Most

I would like to take this opportunity to tell you how much impact you have made in my life. I’ve known you for a long time and losing you since then was a great loss that I still haven’t fully adjusted from. Truth be told, I think I’m fine with admitting that I haven’t made an effort to move on from you because of the reason that I don’t want to. I still wear the necklace with your ring as a pendant everyday in hopes of another miracle might occur, who knows? Maybe we could get back together. That’s always the thought inside my head.

I also want to tell you how severe my burnout is. I can barely keep my shit together (lol don’t mind the lies I told you in the author section) but I can still keep it together nonetheless. The past 3 months were the worst and I kept on contacting you in hopes of feeling better. I’m telling you this because I’m comfortable with you knowing the shit I’m dealing with and I don’t mean to make you feel bad with this. I just want you to be there and listen, as a friend, stranger, an ex, I don’t know. All I know is that in your presence is where I find my comfort and peace.

I mentioned that this is the culmination of my feelings, and it is. This is me telling you my desires and what I want for the both of us. I want to spend my future with you. I want to see you graduate and succeed in life. I want to be there in your lowest and celebrate with you in your highest. I want you also to be there for me, to be my comfort in trying times and the one I first look for whenever I achieve a goal in life. This is me now thinking that I’ve grown myself enough to consider myself worthy of telling you again the words I love you. Losing you made me realize how incomplete my life was without you. You are the only person in this world who truly compliments me. This is me not saying I need you but instead how much I want you.

I made this with the intent of showing you how genuine and sincere my feelings are. I know that you are also dealing with a lot of things right now but I want you to know that I understand. I will always understand you and be patient with you.
